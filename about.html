Automobili Lamborghini

S.p.A. or simply Lamborghini is an Italian automotive company that produces only high-end sports cars. Its headquarters is located in Sant’Agata Bolognese, near the city of Bologna, Italy. The Italian engineer Feruccio Lamborghini founded it in 1963.
